Winter Examination Session

Exam rules

Test takers arrive in front of the examination room at least 30 minutes before the exam starts. Due to COVID-19, the test taker comes to the exam with a face mask and can bring drinking water to the examination room. (according to the guidelines related to COVID-19, the school does not provide drinking water) Each test taker in the written part using the test sheets and answer cards should have a pen (pen) with black ink for marking solutions, and may have simple calculator * (according to the COVID-19 guidelines, the school does not provide pens or pens and calculators).

* Simple calculator - is a calculator that allows you to perform only addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, possibly calculating percentages or square roots from numbers.
